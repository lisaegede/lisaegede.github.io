Hello!

My name is Lisa Egede.

I'm a 3rd year PhD student in the Human Computer Interaction Institute (HCII) at Carnegie Mellon University where I'm advised by Geoff Kaufman.

My research interests center around understanding how historically underrepresented communities experience and utilize technology. Through my research I want advance our current understanding of how to design socially responsible technology through participatory design and community based approaches.

In my free time I enjoy riding my bike, making spotify playlists and taking pictures on my film camera!

You can reach me at lisaegede96 [at] gmail.com!

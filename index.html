My name is Lisa and I'm a PhD candidate in the Human Computer Interaction Institute (HCII) at Carnegie Mellon University. I recieved my undergraduate degree in Computer Science from the University of Oklahoma.

Grounded in societal computing, my research is oriented around understanding how diverse user groups interpret and trust AI systems. My research has been published at various conference venues including CHI, DIS and CSCW!

In my free time I enjoy making Spotify playlists and taking pictures on my film camera.
